### [1] [SeedProteo: Accurate De Novo All-Atom Design of Protein Binders](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24192)
*Qu Wei,Ma Yiming,Ye Fei,Lu Chan,Zhou Yi,Zhang Kexin,Wang Lan,Gui Minrui,Gu Quanquan*

Main category: q-bio.BM

TL;DR: SeedProteo是基于扩散模型的蛋白质设计工具，通过整合自条件特征实现高效设计


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 提出SeedProteo，旨在将前沿的折叠架构转化为生成式蛋白质设计框架

Method: 利用扩散模型和自条件特征，适用于无条件生成和结合剂设计

Result: 模型在无条件生成中表现优异，结合剂设计达到开源方法中的最佳性能

Conclusion: SeedProteo展示了在蛋白质设计中的强大能力和多样性

Abstract: We present SeedProteo, a diffusion-based model for de novo all-atom protein design. We demonstrate how to repurpose a cutting-edge folding architecture into a powerful generative design framework by effectively integrating self-conditioning features. Extensive benchmarks highlight the model's capabilities across two distinct tasks: in unconditional generation, SeedProteo exhibits superior length generalization and structural diversity, maintaining robustness for long sequences and complex topologies; in binder design, it achieves state-of-the-art performance among open-source methods, attaining the highest in-silico design success rates, structural diversity and novelty.

### [2] [SeedFold: Scaling Biomolecular Structure Prediction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24354)
*Zhou Yi,Lu Chan,Ma Yiming,Qu Wei,Ye Fei,Zhang Kexin,Wang Lan,Gui Minrui,Gu Quanquan*

Main category: q-bio.BM

TL;DR: SeedFold是一种高效的蛋白质折叠模型，通过扩展模型容量和改进计算方法，性能优于AlphaFold3。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 构建高精度的生物分子结构预测模型是开发生物分子基础模型的关键，而扩展模型的方法是其中的核心挑战。

Method: 1. 提出Pairformer的有效宽度扩展策略；2. 引入线性三角注意力降低计算复杂度；3. 构建大规模蒸馏数据集。

Result: 在FoldBench上的实验表明，SeedFold在多数蛋白质相关任务中优于AlphaFold3。

Conclusion: SeedFold通过创新的扩展策略和高效计算方法，显著提升了蛋白质结构预测的性能。

Abstract: Highly accurate biomolecular structure prediction is a key component of developing biomolecular foundation models, and one of the most critical aspects of building foundation models is identifying the recipes for scaling the model. In this work, we present SeedFold, a folding model that successfully scales up the model capacity. Our contributions are threefold: first, we identify an effective width-scaling strategy for the Pairformer to increase representation capacity; second, we introduce a novel linear triangular attention that reduces computational complexity to enable efficient scaling; finally, we construct a large-scale distillation dataset to substantially enlarge the training set. Experiments on FoldBench show that SeedFold outperforms AlphaFold3 on most protein-related tasks.

</details>
