### [1] [Network Characteristics of Individual Pigments in Cyanobacterial Photosystem II Core Complexes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17096)
*Eun Lee,Petter Holme*

Main category: q-bio.BM

TL;DR: 摘要研究了光系统II（PSII）中激发能量传递（EET）与色素相互作用结构的关联，通过网络分析揭示了网络拓扑结构的功能相关性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了理解EET与色素相互作用结构的关联，尤其是PSII核心复合体的EET功能是否可以通过网络拓扑结构解释。

Method: 基于Förster理论构建EET路径网络，并通过计算中介中心性、特征向量中心性和加权聚类等网络结构指标进行分析。

Result: 网络分析再现了已知特性，表明网络拓扑编码了功能信息，例如CP47中的叶绿素分子具有更高的能量传递概率，反应中心活性分支叶绿素具有高特征向量中心性和中介中心性。

Conclusion: 网络拓扑结构能够有效反映PSII核心复合体中EET的功能相关性，为理解色素相互作用提供了新视角。

Abstract: Part of the excitation energy transfer (EET) characteristics of the photosystem II (PSII) comes from the interconnection between pigments. To understand the correlation between the EET and the pigments' interaction structure, we construct a network from the EET rates, which are related to both the distance between the pigments (chlorophylls and pheophytins) and their spatial orientations. Especially, we investigate how well the PS II core complex's EET functionality can be explained by using only the network topology in Thermosynechococcus vulcanus 1.9 Å. Starting from the Förster theory, we construct a network of EET pathways. For an analysis of the network structure, we calculate common network-structural measures like betweenness centrality, eigenvector centrality and weighted clustering. These measures can reflect the role of individual pigments in the EET network. In our work, we found that some well-known properties were reproduced by the network analysis of the simplified network, which means that the topology of the network encodes functionally relevant information. For example, from the network structural analysis, we can infer that most of the chlorophyll molecules (chlorophylls) in the pigment-protein complex CP47 have a heightened probability of transferring energy compared with other chlorophylls. We also see that the active branch chlorophylls in the reaction center are characterized by a high eigenvector centrality, a high betweenness centrality, and a low weighted clustering coefficient. This is indicative of functionally important vertices.

### [2] [The energy landscape of DNA-binding proteins along the genome](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17753)
*Alessandro Pandolfi,Riccardo Beccaria,Guido Tiana*

Main category: q-bio.BM

TL;DR: 开发一种机器学习算法来预测DNA结合蛋白的能量分布，并验证其有效性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 需要高效量化DNA结合蛋白的结合自由能，以重建其基因组上的能量分布。

Method: 利用蛋白质结构和DNA结合位点数据集训练机器学习算法，学习蛋白质界面和DNA序列的潜在不变表示，并预测自由能。

Result: 成功验证了方法，并将其用于预测单域转录因子（PU.1）在哺乳动物染色体上的结合区域及其稳定性。

Conclusion: 该方法能够有效预测DNA结合蛋白的能量分布，为研究其结合稳定性提供新工具。

Abstract: Reconstructing the energy profile of DNA-binding proteins along the genome requires an algorithm that quantifies efficiently the binding free energy. We assembled a dataset of protein structures and DNA binding sites, together with their binding energies, and used it to train a machine-learning algorithm that learns a latent invariant representation of the protein interface and of the DNA sequence, combining them to predict the free energy. After validating the method, we used it to determine the energy profile of a single-domain transcription factor (PU.1) sliding on mammalian chromosomes, predicting its binding regions and quantifying the statistical properties that determine their stability and their kinetic accessibility.

</details>
